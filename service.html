Our Services

General Treatment

A comprehensive pest management solution to target common household pests like ants, spiders, and flies. This treatment involves preventive measures and surface applications to protect your home from infestations. Ideal for maintaining a pest-free environment year-round.

Rodent Control

Our rodent control service eliminates rats and mice that pose health risks and damage property. We use advanced techniques, including traps, baits, and exclusion methods, to remove rodents and prevent re-entry.

Bed Bug Control

Bed bugs can disrupt your sleep and cause discomfort. Our specialized bed bug treatment includes thorough inspections, heat treatments, and eco-friendly chemical applications to eliminate these pests and prevent recurrence.

Sanitation

A clean environment is essential to keep pests at bay. Our sanitation services focus on eliminating breeding grounds for pests, such as food debris and moisture. We also provide guidance on maintaining hygiene to deter future infestations.

Termite Control Treatment

Termites can cause severe structural damage to your property. Our termite control services include soil treatment, wood preservation, and advanced baiting systems to eradicate termites and protect your home from further damage.

Cockroach Control

Cockroaches are a common nuisance and health hazard. Our control methods target breeding sites using gel baits, sprays, and preventive treatments, ensuring your space remains cockroach-free.

Mosquito Control

Protect your family from mosquito-borne diseases like dengue and malaria with our mosquito control service. We use fogging, larvicide applications, and environmental management to reduce mosquito populations effectively.

Honey Bee Control

Honey bees can be a danger when they nest near your home. Our bee control services focus on safely removing hives without harming the bees, following eco-friendly practices and ensuring your safety.
